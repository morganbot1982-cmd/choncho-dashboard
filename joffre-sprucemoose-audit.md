## The Pathway

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1–2)

**Objective:** Get online with a professional presence that can take money.

- [ ] Choose and register a domain name (e.g. sprucemoose.art, sprucemooseart.com)
- [ ] Set up website — portfolio + online store + mailing list signup
  - **Recommended platform:** Shopify (best for selling) or Squarespace (best portfolio/store hybrid for artists)
  - Alternative: BigCartel (free tier, artist-focused, simpler)
- [ ] Photograph or export your best 20–30 pieces in high resolution
- [ ] Write an About page (your story, your style, 15 years of craft — this sells)
- [ ] Set up print-on-demand integration (Printful or Prodigi) — no stock risk
- [ ] Connect Instagram to website
- [ ] Set up a free mailing list (Mailchimp or Kit — formerly ConvertKit)

**Budget estimate:** $150–$300 (domain + first year hosting/platform)

### Phase 4 — Growth & Marketing (Month 2–3)

**Objective:** Drive traffic to the store and build an audience you own.

#### Instagram Strategy
- Post 4–5 times per week
- **Content that works for graffiti/comic artists:**
  - Time-lapse process videos (Procreate + spray can footage)
  - Before/after pieces
  - WIP (work in progress) shots
  - Behind-the-scenes at walls/studios
  - Reel tutorials (short "how I draw X" clips)
- Use relevant hashtags: #graffitiart #comicart #brisbaneart #streetart #illustration
- Engage with other artists and potential buyers daily (15 min/day)

#### Email List
- Offer a free digital download (wallpaper, print-ready art) as signup incentive
- Send monthly newsletter: new work, behind-the-scenes, upcoming shows
- This is your insurance against algorithm changes

#### Local Presence (Brisbane)
- Apply to local art markets (Brisbane Art & Design Market, Boundary Street Markets, Jan Powers Farmers Markets — they often have art stalls)
- Approach local cafes/bars about hanging work (commission on sales)
- Connect with Brisbane street art community for mural commissions

#### SEO (Website)
- Target keywords: "Brisbane artist", "graffiti art prints", "comic art prints Australia", "custom character design"
- Blog/journal on the site with your process — drives organic traffic
