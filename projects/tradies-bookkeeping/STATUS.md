# AI Bookkeeping Service (Tradies) - Project Status

**Last Updated:** March 11, 2026  
**Status:** Active - Research & planning phase

## What This Is
AI-powered bookkeeping service for Australian tradies using WhatsApp as the primary interface. Zero friction receipt capture → AI processing → Xero posting → monthly human review.

## Target Market
- Brisbane tradies (plumbers, electricians, builders)
- $200K-$1M annual revenue
- Currently doing own books OR paying $800+/month for accountant
- Hate paperwork, want clean books

## Core Value Proposition
- **Zero friction:** WhatsApp = instant receipt capture (no app download)
- **Accuracy:** AI + human review = trust without full automation risk
- **Real-time data:** Track expenses as they happen, not monthly
- **Cost savings:** $397-$897/mo vs $800+ for traditional bookkeeper
