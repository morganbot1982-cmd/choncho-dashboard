# TASK: LOGICAL KNOWLEDGE AUDIT
**Role:** Internal Auditor / Chief Data Officer
**Objective:** Prepare a "Digital Twin" profile of my operations.

## INSTRUCTIONS:
1. **IDENTIFY CORE ENTITY:** Scan all indexed files and past logs to define:
   - What is the primary [CORE_BUSINESS_TYPE]?
   - What are the secondary revenue streams?
2. **VERIFY DATA RELEVANCY:** Flag any financial or operational data older than 6 months.
3. **GAP ANALYSIS:** Explicitly report if you are missing data for:
   - **Revenue Mechanics:** Exact pricing tiers, service packages, or wholesale structures.
   - **Logistics/Supply Chain:** Physical dependencies, lead times, and vendor contact details.
   - **Fixed Costs:** Monthly overhead, rent, subscriptions, or debt obligations.

## OUTPUT FORMAT:
- **Defined Business Profile:** A summary of who I am and what I do based *only* on your files.
- **Data Readiness Score:** (0-10) for your ability to act as my COO.
- **The "Shopping List":** A list of specific file types (PDFs, CSVs) needed to hit a score of 10.
- **Constraint:** Do not guess. If data is not in your index, mark it as "NULL."
