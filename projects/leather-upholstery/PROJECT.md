# Leather Projects and Work

**Status:** Planning  
**Priority:** MEDIUM  
**Started:** 2026-02-28

## Goal

Set up for leather upholstery work - equipment, materials, workspace setup.

## Shopping List - Essential Tools

### Cutting Tools
- [ ] Rotary cutter (45mm or 60mm)
- [ ] Self-healing cutting mat (A2 or larger)
- [ ] Leather shears/scissors (heavy duty)
- [ ] Craft knife (X-Acto or similar)
- [ ] Replacement blades

### Stitching Tools
- [ ] Stitching awl (diamond or round point)
- [ ] Harness needles (size 00-3, pack of 10)
- [ ] Waxed thread (nylon or polyester, multiple weights)
- [ ] Thread conditioner/beeswax
- [ ] Stitching pony or clamp (holds leather while stitching)
- [ ] Pricking irons/chisels (2mm, 3mm, 4mm spacing)
- [ ] Mallet or maul (rawhide or nylon)

### Edge Finishing
- [ ] Edge beveler (size 1-3)
- [ ] Slicker/burnisher (wood or bone)
- [ ] Gum tragacanth or Tokonole (edge finish)
- [ ] Sandpaper (150, 220, 400, 600 grit)

### Hardware & Fasteners
- [ ] Rivets (various sizes: 6mm, 9mm, 12mm)
- [ ] Rivet setter kit
- [ ] Snap setter kit + snaps (line 20, line 24)
- [ ] Buckles (various sizes)
- [ ] D-rings and O-rings

### Adhesives & Finishes
- [ ] Contact cement (Barge or Weldwood)
- [ ] Leather dye (Fiebing's or Angelus)
- [ ] Resolene or finish coat
- [ ] Daubers/applicators

### Measuring & Marking
- [ ] Wing divider (adjustable spacing)
- [ ] Steel ruler (12" and 24")
- [ ] T-square
- [ ] Scratch compass
- [ ] White pencil or silver pen

### Workspace
- [ ] Work bench or sturdy table
- [ ] Cutting board/granite slab
- [ ] Storage bins/organizers
- [ ] Good lighting (task lamp)
- [ ] Ventilation (for dye/adhesive fumes)

## Shopping List - Materials

### Leather
- [ ] Veg-tan leather (tooling leather, 6-8oz)
- [ ] Chrome-tan leather (upholstery weight, 3-5oz)
- [ ] Practice scrap pieces
- [ ] Sample pack (different colors/finishes)

### Supplies
- [ ] Leather conditioner (neatsfoot oil or similar)
- [ ] Rags/cloths
- [ ] Gloves (nitrile for dye work)
- [ ] Apron

## Budget Estimate

### Starter Kit (Essentials)
- Tools: $300-500
- Materials (leather + thread): $150-200
- Hardware: $50-100
- **Total:** ~$500-800 AUD

### Professional Setup
- Tools: $800-1500
- Materials: $300-500
- Workspace upgrades: $200-400
- **Total:** ~$1300-2400 AUD
