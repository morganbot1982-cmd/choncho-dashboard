# Clarat — Competitive Analysis (March 2026)

## Summary

Clarat sits in the e-commerce P&L / profitability space. The main competitors are SaaS tools that charge monthly subscriptions based on order volume or GMV. Clarat's differentiation: **one-time purchase, zero formulas, instant driver-based recalculation, and scenario modelling.**

---

## Competitor Breakdown

### Triple Whale
**What they do:** Full-stack e-commerce analytics — attribution, pixel tracking, P&L, creative analytics, cohort analysis, AI chat.

**Pricing (monthly, annual commitment):**
| Plan | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Free | $0 | Basic tracking, 12-month lookback, 10 users |
| Starter | $179/mo | Multi-touch attribution, Sonar Send, unlimited lookback |
| Advanced | $259/mo | Creative/product analytics, cohort analysis, profitability, BI tools |
| Custom | $539+/mo | Enterprise, $50M+ GMV |

**Strengths:** Deep attribution (Triple Pixel), massive integration ecosystem, AI chat (Moby), creative analytics, well-funded.
**Weaknesses:** Expensive for small stores, complex feature set, attribution-focused (profitability is secondary), subscription model.

**Clarat angle:** Triple Whale is for funded brands doing $1M+. Clarat is for the bootstrapped store owner who just wants to know daily profit without paying $179+/mo.

---

### Lifetimely → Amp (rebranded)
**What they do:** LTV-focused analytics — daily P&L, customer lifetime value, cohort analysis, forecasting, attribution.

**Pricing (monthly):**
| Plan | Price | Orders/mo |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Free | $0 | 50 orders |
| M | $149/mo | Up to 3,000 |
| L | $299/mo | Up to 7,000 |
| XL | $499/mo | Up to 15,000 (most popular) |
| XXL | $749/mo | Up to 25,000 |
| Unlimited | $999/mo | Unlimited |
| Amazon Add-on | +$75/mo | Amazon data import |

**All plans include all features** — pricing is purely volume-based.

**Features:** Daily P&L, LTV with predictive AI, CAC analysis, customer journey, custom dashboards, attribution, sales forecasting, what-if scenarios, AI chat.

**Strengths:** LTV expertise, customer journey tracking, predictive AI, clean UI.
**Weaknesses:** Expensive at scale ($499-999/mo), LTV focus means P&L is deep but not the star, Shopify-only.

**Clarat angle:** Lifetimely/Amp charges $149-999/mo. Clarat gives you daily P&L + scenario modelling as a one-time buy. No LTV tracking (yet) but the P&L is more immediate and driver-based.

---

### BeProfit
**What they do:** Shopify profit analytics — real-time profit dashboard, order-level profitability, product analytics, shipping cost tracking.

**Pricing:** (Unable to fetch — likely $20-50/mo based on prior research, Shopify app store model)

**Features:** Per-order profit calculation, COGS tracking, shipping cost rules, marketing spend tracking, custom reports.

**Strengths:** Shopify native, affordable, focused on profitability.
**Weaknesses:** Shopify-only, less sophisticated scenario modelling, no LTV, no attribution.

**Clarat angle:** Most similar competitor in terms of focus (daily profitability). Clarat differentiates with driver-based instant recalculation, Dream Month scenario modelling, and one-time pricing.

## Clarat's Positioning

### Why Clarat Wins

| Feature | Triple Whale | Amp | BeProfit | Clarat |
|---------|-------------|-----|---------|--------|
| Daily P&L | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Driver-based recalc | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Scenario modelling | ❌ | Basic | ❌ | ✅ Dream Month |
| One-time pricing | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| No Shopify lock-in | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ (CSV import) |
| Cost per month | $179-539 | $149-999 | ~$20-50 | $0 after purchase |
| Setup complexity | High | Medium | Low | Low |

### Target Customer
- Bootstrapped e-commerce store doing $100K-$2M/year
- Using Shopify, WooCommerce, or manual entry
- Wants real profit visibility without $200+/mo SaaS
- Cares about "what if I negotiate cheaper shipping?" → instant answer

### Pricing Suggestion
- **Starter:** $99 one-time (1 store, all 6 views, CSV import)
- **Pro:** $199 one-time (multi-store, Shopify auto-import, email alerts)
- **Team:** $349 one-time (unlimited users, shared drivers, priority support)

These are dramatically cheaper than competitors while still profitable (zero marginal cost after build).

### Key Risks
1. **Shopify app ecosystem** — competitors are embedded in Shopify. Clarat needs CSV or API import to compete.
2. **Attribution gap** — Triple Whale's pixel is a moat. Clarat doesn't do attribution (intentionally).
3. **LTV gap** — Amp/Lifetimely's LTV predictions are valuable. Could add later.
4. **Self-hosted vs cloud** — Clarat is currently self-hosted. Needs cloud deployment for mass adoption.
